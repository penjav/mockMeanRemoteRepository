About Us

February 2017, was the birth of mockingCode. It aims for all the small business, enthusiast writers, artists and photographers to have their own website in order to improve their business. On top of that we create simple web-application for institutions such as different communities in the city around the world. As well as for non-profit organizations and for personal purposes.

In a nutshell, what mockingCode does is code and deploy finished site for you. That means, it uses all the necessary tools and languages to build your web-app.
